The Best Movies According To Arvindhra Rao Krishnamurthy

All-time favourites of mine!!!

Jersey

A movie on an aging familyman who was once a superstar in the cricket and had to
retire at a young age due to unfortunate circumstances and had to make a comeback.
upon his son's request.

This is a movie based on the losers who had to give up their dreams and hopes
due to various reasons and tries to make a comeback against the odds stacked up
against them.
